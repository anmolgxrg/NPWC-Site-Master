INSURANCES ACCEPTED

NeuroPsych Wellness Center, PC is in network with most of the commercial insurance companies. We will try our best to make your financing process go as smoothly as possible.

Our services are currently covered by Medicare, Anthem, Carefirst PPO, Carefirst HMO, United Behavioral Health, Cigna, Aetna, Humana Tricare Military, and Optima.

Some of our patients decide to self-pay rather than billing their insurance.
